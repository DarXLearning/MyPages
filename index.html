The Prophet said:

"Whoever fasts Ramadan, then follows it up with 6 fasts in Shawwāl, it is as if he has fasted the whole year."

Shahih Muslim [1164]
Al-Hafidh al-Nawawi رحمه الله said: "The scholars have said it is as if one has fasted the whole year because good deeds are multiplied 10 fold; so Ramadan equals 10 months and 6 days (from Shawwal) equals 2 months." Al-Minhaj 4/297 Shaykh Muhammad Ibn Şalih al-Uthaymin رحمه الله said: "It is better that the six fasts from Shawwal are consecutive and that they are directly after 'Eid al-Fitr; due to what that contains from hastening to good." Fatāwā Nūr 'alā al-Darb, cassette no: 239
